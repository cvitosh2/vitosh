Translation of Genealogy research - by Otto Vitous

We are sending you (for your request from 20.9. 1999) following extracts from the register and other informations:


1. Parish register of births of Roman Catholic rectory in Stupno, vol.6 (foil 519)

   Date:  8.3. 1866(!)

   Place: Krise No. 16

   Name: Josef

Father: Vitous Jan Baptist, cottier in Krise, son of + Frantisek Vitous   (cottier in Krise No.16),
and of mother Ann (maiden name Auterska borned in Dolnostupna No.17 (=Stupno))

Mother: Anna, daughter  of Vojtech Blecha (peasant in Krise) and of mother + Anna (maiden name Razimova      borned in Mostice no.2)

+ means that they were already dead in the time of record

Brothers and sisters of Josef Vitous:

    (dd.mm.yy)
a) 10. 11. 1854 -  Katerina (Register-Stupno 5/575) = Catherine

b) 7. 3. 1857 -  Vaclav   (Register -Stupno 6/56)  = Wenceslas

c) 25. 1. 1859 - Matej (Register -Stupno 6/137) = Matthias

d) 7. 5. 1861 - Vojtech (Register -Stupno 6/241) = Adalbert

e) 21. 7. 1863 - Marie (Register -Stupno 6/364) = Mary

We indicate that the said dates of born of brothers and sisters (your  addition from 28.2. 2000) are mostly wrong. Vojtech = Albert/Adalbert, Vaclav  was often changed in the american setting to Jakub (James)

  Place: St. Cross No.13 =Sv. Kriz

  Name: Jan Baptist Vitous

  Father: Frantisek Vittaus, cottier from Sv. Kriz, son of Vaclav  Vittaus (peasant in Stupno)
  and of mother + Katerina (maiden name Kozeluhova

  Mother: Anna daughter of Frantisek Autersky (pension   in  kind from Krise ) and of mother Barbora
  (maiden name Nausova)   


  Brothers and sisters of Jan Baptist Vitous:

  (dd.mm.yy)
a) 3. 2. 1824  - Barbora (Register-Radnice 11/357) = Barbra  
                      child waas acquire after parents wedding.

b) 23. 8. 1828 - Maria (Register -Radnice 11/366) = Mary

c) 28. 2. 1831 - Josephus (Register -Radnice 11/374), +

d) 27. 12. 1834 - Josefa = girl (Register- Stupno 3/59-60)

e) 2. 6. 1837 - Matej (Register -Stupno 4/116) = Matthias

f) 23. 6. 1840 - Josef (Register -Stupno 5/4/127) = Joseph

g) 16. 5. 1844 - Katerina (Register- Stupno 5/440-441)


It is clear (from these information) that Josef Vitous (borned 1.4.  1848)  was not a brother of Jan Baptist (Krtitel).  In the same  time several  Vitous' family lived  and have children in the Parishes  of Stupno and  Radnice.

We indicate that searching in the way to the present, conseqently  for living members of family is very time and money consuming and it is not  often succesful. There mostly were not any notices  in the birth recores about later  moving, marriages etc. In the case of your interest we can try to find  datas about  other destinies of Jan Baptist (Krtitel) sisters and brothers.  But  you must take account of  long lasting backround research with  uncertain result. It is charge of  CZK 260 - CZK 380 per hour  only  of researching not for found record ( even in the case of unsuccessful  result). This charge tax is counted  even of negative result.
